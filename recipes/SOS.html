S.O.S. (SHIT ON A SHINGLE)

Classic Comfort Food

The S.O.S. (Shit on a Shingle) is a simple and satisfying dish made with creamed chipped beef on toast. It's a nostalgic favorite for many, known for its ease of preparation and comforting flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup dried chipped beef
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of toast
  • Optional: Chopped parsley or chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the chipped beef and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the chipped beef and stir to coat.
  4. Slowly whisk in the milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toast the bread slices to your desired level of doneness.
  7. Spoon the creamed chipped beef mixture over the toast.
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley or chives, if desired.
