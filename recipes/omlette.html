WESTERN OMELETTE

Classic Western Omelette

The Western Omelette is a hearty and flavorful breakfast dish featuring a combination of eggs, ham, vegetables, and cheese. It's a popular choice for its ease of preparation and satisfying taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Optional: Chopped chives or parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and pepper to the pan and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the ham and cook for another minute.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it cook until the edges begin to set.
  6. Once the bottom is mostly set, sprinkle the cheese over one half of the omelette.
  7. Using a spatula, gently fold the unfilled side over the cheese to create a half-moon shape.
  8. Cook for another minute or two, until the cheese is melted and the omelette is cooked through.
  9. Slide the omelette onto a plate and garnish with chopped chives or parsley, if desired.
